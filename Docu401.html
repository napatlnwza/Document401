This is a website that compiles information about the 16th class of the Science, Mathematics, Technology and Environment Special Class at Sanpatong Wittayakhom School.

What is SMTE?

S : Science

M : Mathematics

T : Technology

E : Environment

It is a project that focuses on developing the potential of students with special abilities in science, mathematics, technology and the environment by IPST.

Instruction:

SMTE classroom instruction emphasizes the integration of knowledge across multiple disciplines, particularly science, mathematics, technology, and the environment, enabling students to apply their knowledge to real-world problem-solving.

Benefits:

Students who study in SMTE classrooms benefit greatly, including learning in a supportive learning environment, the opportunity to participate in a variety of activities, the development of skills necessary for further study, and the opportunity to pursue further studies in faculties and fields related to science, mathematics, technology, and the environment.
